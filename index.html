League of Legends is a multiplayer online battle arena video game developed and published by Riot Games for Microsoft Windows and macOS. The game was released on October 27, 2009. It has become popular and has built competitive environments. Riot Games organizes regional tournaments. These regional leagues select the best regional teams to compete in the Mid-Season Invitational and Worlds.
